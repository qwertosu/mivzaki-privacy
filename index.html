Privacy Policy - Mivzaki

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

The "Mivzaki" application (hereinafter: "the App") respects user privacy. This privacy policy explains what data is collected, how it is stored, and how it is used.

Information Collected

Anonymous usage data: The App uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as: screen views, searches, source filtering, favorites usage, and settings. This data does not include any personally identifiable information.

Crash data: The App uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports in order to improve app stability.

Advertising: The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect information in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Local preferences: Settings such as theme, favorites, and notification preferences are stored locally on the device only and are not sent to any server.

Information Not Collected

The App does not collect: name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any other personally identifiable information.

Data Storage

All preferences and favorites are stored locally on the device. Anonymous usage data is stored on Google's Firebase servers in accordance with Firebase's Terms of Service.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following services:

  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics
  • Google AdMob

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new update date.
